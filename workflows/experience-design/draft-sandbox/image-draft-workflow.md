# Image Draft Workflow

Image/logo/icon/illustration generations are drafts until reviewed and approved.

## Draft storage

- Failed or exploratory generations: `local-only/drafts/image-generations/`
- Candidate assets: `design-assets/pending/`
- Approved assets: `design-assets/approved/`

## Promotion requirements

Before promotion, check:

- visual fit;
- licensing/source status if external;
- format and resolution;
- platform usage;
- Figma import needs;
- whether the asset is referenced by a screen/story.
