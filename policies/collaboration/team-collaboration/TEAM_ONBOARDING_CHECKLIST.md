# Team Onboarding Checklist

- Add team member to TEAM_MEMBER_REGISTRY.
- Assign roles in ROLE_ASSIGNMENT_REGISTRY.
- Define approval authority.
- Identify needed repo/tool access.
- Invite to GitHub/Figma/Trello/Supabase as needed.
- Generate role context pack.
- Confirm secrets policy for this member.
- Record onboarding approval.
