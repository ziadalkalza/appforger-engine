# Workflow Impact Analysis Checklist

Before accepting a skip, substitution, reorder, or import, answer:

- What stage is affected?
- Is this a skip, substitute, reorder, import, draft, or waiver?
- What artifact replaces the normal output?
- Which baselines are affected?
- Which registries must change?
- Which tasks/packets become stale?
- Are security/privacy/release rules affected?
- Are team approvals required?
- Are conflicts likely?
- What evidence is missing?
- What is the safest path forward?
