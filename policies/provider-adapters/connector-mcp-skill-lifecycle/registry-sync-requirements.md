# Registry Sync Requirements

When a connector, MCP, skill, or integration changes state, update the relevant registry:

- `registries/project-control/connector-lifecycle-registry.md`
- `registries/project-control/mcp-lifecycle-registry.md`
- `registries/project-control/skill-lifecycle-registry.md`
- `registries/project-control/connector-registry.md`
- `registries/project-control/mcp-registry.md`
- `policies/governance/cost-dependencies/service-dependency-registry.md`

A capability is not considered active until the registry says active and the validation evidence is recorded.
