# Flexible Workflow Policy

The default Appforger lifecycle remains recommended, but not mandatory.

A workflow may be changed if:
1. the user explicitly requests the change or accepts Appforger's proposed variant;
2. Appforger records the selected variant;
3. Appforger performs an impact analysis;
4. missing evidence is tracked;
5. non-negotiable rules remain active;
6. project-control is updated;
7. downstream packets state the real status of skipped, substituted, imported, draft, or pending stages.

Flexible workflow does not mean uncontrolled work. It means controlled deviation from the recommended stage order.
