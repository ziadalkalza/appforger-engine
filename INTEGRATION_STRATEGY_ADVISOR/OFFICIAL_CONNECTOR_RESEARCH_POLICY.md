# Official Connector Research Policy

Connector ecosystems change quickly. Before recommending or creating an integration for an external service, the acting model should research current official/trusted connector, plugin, MCP, or marketplace options.

The research should identify:

- official provider connector/plugin/MCP;
- supported clients/tools;
- read vs write capabilities;
- authentication model;
- scope/permission controls;
- enterprise/admin controls;
- maintenance status;
- cost/plan requirements;
- whether it supports the user's goal.

Research findings should be summarized in the integration decision packet and logged in `project-control/integrations/integration-decision-log.md`.

If the acting environment cannot browse, it must say that connector availability needs verification before finalizing connector/API/custom-code decisions.
