# Google Drive Connector

## Role

Use Google Drive for:

- Product docs.
- Reference images/files.
- Exported design assets.
- Client-provided requirements.
- Manual handoff material.

## Rules

- Treat Drive as a source/handoff location, not the only source of truth.
- Summaries or extracted details must be copied into AppForge docs/registries.
- Do not assume Drive files are current unless checked.

## Handoff

Record:

- File name.
- Link.
- Version/date.
- What was imported into AppForge.
- What remains external.
