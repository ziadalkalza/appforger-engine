# Design to Frontend Handoff

Use this packet to transfer responsibility between roles.

Required fields:
- handoff_id
- from_role
- to_role
- related feature/story/task
- source artifacts
- approved baselines
- unresolved questions
- known risks
- required next action
- required approval/evidence

Must include Figma baseline, approved frames, components, tokens, assets, states, motion notes, and implementation notes.
